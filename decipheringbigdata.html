About the Module

This Module explored the concepts of big data, as well as the data pipeline as a whole. It covered subjects such as data types, sources and methods of collecting data, cleaning and processing of data and Database modelling/design. This page will show some of the artefacts created during the course as well explore some of my thoughts on my professional development.

Collaborative Discussions

Two Collaborative discussions took place during the module. A Collaborative Discussion is where all students participating in the module would post on a forum and discuss relevant topics to gain a better understanding of the topic. Discussion one was on the Data Collection Process, and Discussion 2 was on Comparing Compliance Laws.
