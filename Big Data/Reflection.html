Reflection

Through the duration of this module I have gained a broader and more complete view of how data is handled within businesses as well as the processes involved. I alsready had some experience with Exploratory data analysis and cleaning data for use in product or for accurate analysis, however this module has helped me fit that into the complete data pipeline.

The most impactful thing learned for my proffessional goals would be the design and review of Database modelling and different use cases for each model. The course went through a comprehensive process of database modelling based around customer expectations and requirements.

I got more practice with SQL and learned about RDBMS systems and NoSQL systems, as well as the benefits and limitations of each. Working on the Team Projects such as Database design team project has helped me realise what designing and implementing a database encompasses and how vital each member of a development team is for completion of a project.
